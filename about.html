Daija's Bridal Boutique

Welcome to Daija's Bridal Boutique Established in 1996. Family owned founded by Elizabeth Ruvalcaba. Elizabeth started this business because her love for happy endings and helping women find the dresses of their dreams. She named the boutique after her daughter Daija. Please stop by and get ready to find the dress of your DREAMS!

We know planning for a wedding can be so stressful as it is. More importantly for a women to find the dress of her dreams. We here at Daija's boutique understand the stress and the important choice for everyone women to walk out of our boutique with the dress of their dreams. We have prices for all ranges of wedding budgets so don't worry about the price being a big factor we can help you get the dress without hurting the wedding budget to much!
